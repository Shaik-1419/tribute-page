About Dr. Kalam

Dr. Avul Pakir Jainulabdeen Abdul Kalam, known as the **Missile Man of India**, was an eminent scientist and leader. Born on **15th October 1931**, he played a crucial role in India's **space and missile development programs**.

He served as the **11th President of India** from **2002 to 2007**, winning millions of hearts with his humility, wisdom, and vision for a developed India.

Achievements

  • Developed India's first **indigenous satellite launch vehicle (SLV)**
  • Key figure in the **Pokhran-II nuclear tests** (1998)
  • Authored **"Wings of Fire"**, an inspiring autobiography
  • Recipient of India's highest honor, the **Bharat Ratna**

Legacy

Dr. Kalam's impact on education, technology, and national development is **immortal**. Even after his passing on **27th July 2015**, his teachings continue to inspire young minds across the world.
